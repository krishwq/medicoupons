NEET PG 2026 Exam Pattern

  • Mode of Exam: Online (CBT)
  • Duration: 3 hours 30 minutes (210 minutes)
  • Total Questions: 200 MCQs
  • Maximum Marks: 800
  • Language: English only
  • Marking Scheme: +4 for correct answer
  • Negative Marking: -1 for incorrect answer
  • Unattempted: 0 marks

How to Prepare for NEET PG 2026: A Winning Strategy

1. Build a Rock-Solid Foundation

Start with high-yield pre-clinical and para-clinical subjects. For subjects like Physiology, reading standard textbooks like Guyton and Hall or Ganong helps build strong concepts required for clinical questions.

2. Leverage Premium Digital Resources

Platforms like PrepLadder provide structured video lectures, curated QBank and high-yield notes designed specifically for the NEET PG pattern.

3. Maintain a Mistake Ledger

Because of negative marking, accuracy is critical. Maintain a notebook where you record mistakes from mock tests and revise them daily.

4. Spaced Repetition and Grand Tests

Finish the first revision by March 2026. Reserve the remaining months for revision and Grand Tests. Attempt one GT every two weeks initially and weekly near the exam.

FMGE June 2026 Attempt: Complete Guide for Foreign Medical Graduates

Published on March 6, 2026

Every year, thousands of Indian students return home after completing their MBBS abroad with a single dream-to practice medicine in India. But before they can wear the white coat here, they must cross one crucial gateway: the Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE).

If you are planning to appear for the FMGE June 2026 attempt, this guide will walk you through the latest updates, exam schedule, eligibility, pattern, and preparation strategy.

FMGE June 2026: Latest Exam Date & Schedule

According to the National Board of Examinations in Medical Sciences (NBEMS) calendar, the FMGE June 2026 exam will be conducted on 28 June 2026.

The examination will be conducted in two shifts:

  • Shift 1: 9:00 AM – 11:30 AM
  • Shift 2: 2:00 PM – 4:30 PM
  • Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

What is FMGE?

The Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) is a mandatory screening test for Indian citizens or Overseas Citizens of India who complete their medical degree from a foreign university and wish to practice medicine in India.

After qualifying FMGE, candidates can obtain provisional or permanent registration from the National Medical Commission (NMC) or State Medical Councils.

FMGE 2026 Eligibility Criteria

  • Citizenship: Must be an Indian citizen or Overseas Citizen of India (OCI).
  • Medical Qualification: Must possess a primary medical degree equivalent to MBBS from a recognized foreign institution.
  • Degree Completion: The candidate must have completed the medical degree before the cutoff date.
  • Verification Requirement: Degree must be recognized for medical practice in the country where it was awarded.

FMGE Passing Marks

  • Candidates must score 150 out of 300 marks (50%) to qualify.
  • The cutoff score is fixed.
  • No category-wise relaxation is available.

Tips to Crack FMGE June 2026

  • Solve Previous Year Questions regularly.
  • Practice MCQ-based learning because FMGE is purely MCQ driven.
  • Revise multiple times before the exam.
  • Attempt mock tests to improve speed and accuracy.
